RADARS

RADARS is an IoT-based system designed to detect road accidents in remote and mountainous regions, ensuring timely assistance.

It identifies four types of accidents—submersion in water, vehicle fires, collisions, and rollovers—using various sensors.

The system automatically sends distress alerts to hospitals, police stations, ambulances, and victims' families.

Unlike conventional solutions, RADARS operates without internet dependency, utilizing LoRa, GPRS, and GSM for reliable communication.

By providing swift emergency response, RADARS aims to reduce fatalities in accident-prone, isolated areas.
